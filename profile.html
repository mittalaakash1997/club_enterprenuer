JULIE REID PROFILE
GALLUP STRENGTHS: STRATEGIC I LEARNER I ACHIEVER I MAXIMISER I FUTURISTIC

I have over 25 years of experience in strategy, marketing, and research. Over the years, I have developed and evolved an entrepreneurial mindset and approach that creates and grows existing and new opportunities. In all my actions, I am outcome-driven and customer-focused.

My experience is across both the sciences and business disciplines. I started my marketing career with McDonald's Family Restaurants and DDB Advertising, then as a senior marketing executive for an airport group, followed by a property group. I have also delivered research projects for mining giants BHP and Rio Tinto. While completing my MBA (Executive), I went into the higher education sector and further evolved my knowledge and skillsets within entrepreneurship. As a lecturer, I have also developed and delivered entrepreneurship and strategy courses, focusing on industry applications that included hands-on practical skills to ensure success 'beyond the theory'.

My skills and knowledge sets are at the leading edge for creating business value and driving growth.
