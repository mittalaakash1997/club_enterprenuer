COURSE 1
ENTREPRENEURIAL FOUNDATIONS

The first course, Entrepreneurial Foundations, sets you up with entrepreneurial thinking. It is a mix of theory and practical 'hands-on knowledge'. Your live learning sessions will be conducted via an online video collaboration portal or face-to-face, depending on your course enrolment preference.

Your course learning material will be available to you via the 'Entrepreneurship Learning Lab' online portal upon enrollment. The portal is accessible via your desktop and mobile at a time that suits your schedule. However, you will have a timeframe and set times based upon your enrolment slot to do the live learning sessions and complete the first course.

The first-course activity is about understanding who you are in terms of your intuitive strengths. Understanding your ‘strengths’ is essential for your entrepreneurial learning and success story. It is vital to set you up for success in your entrepreneurial adventure. You will also learn how to access critical market research data. Research is a prerequisite to making ideas real.

The course framework follows the following format.

  1. Strength Understanding
  2. Sustainable and Ethical Factors
  3. Research and Design Methodology
  4. Idea Prototype and Build

COURSE 2
ENTREPRENEURIAL IDEA FOCUS

The second course, Entrepreneurial Idea Focus, follows the completion of the Entrepreneurial Foundations (Course 1). Course 2 is when each person can explore the idea you want to make it real. During this process, you will have expert guidance to bring your vision to a minimum prototype form. As you move through this course, your idea will become more vivid. At the end of the period, you will prepare a business pitch to present to people who can support you in your entrepreneurial journey.

COURSE 3
BUSINESS STRATEGY

The third course, Business Strategy, can be completed after completing Courses 1 and 2 (Entrepreneurial Foundations and Entrepreneurial Idea Focus). This course will put you on track to plan and evolve your pathway to win in the market areas that you choose to complete. Understanding how to have a strategic focus is an integral part of business success.

As an outcome of completing the Business Strategy course, you will have the outline of your business strategy plan to launch your new business as an entrepreneur.

COURSE 4
BUSINESS MARKETING

The fourth course in the learning succession is Business Marketing. Understanding the customer and how to segment, position and brand your business is the next critical element required to launch your business. Plan the activities that will attract and engage your target customer segment to form a relationship with your business. Learn how to deliver a customer experience that combines virtual and physical worlds.

As an outcome of completing the Business Marketing course, you will have the outline of your marketing plan to implement as part of your business launch as an entrepreneur.

COURSE 5
INTERNATIONAL STRATEGY

The fifth course in the learning succession is International Strategy. If you are planning to diversify internationally, this is an essential course. Doing business across country borders brings additional risk and great rewards if executed with knowledge. Understand how to plan and make the decisions that will allow your business to compete successfully in a globalised work.

As an outcome of completing the International Strategy course, you will have a clear perspective of what you need to do to cross borders successfully as an entrepreneur.
