ABOUT

During Julie Reid's teaching and involvement in the industry, along with the impact of Covid-19 on lifestyles, careers, and aspirations, Club Entrepreneurship was born. Julie has been involved across industry sectors and has worked at the senior executive level. She has also been teaching in higher education for several years, evolving and developing her knowledge and skillsets across the business, technology, and science streams. Julie is now bringing this knowledge and experience to those that want to make their ideas real.

It is now a reality that the human population outstripped available jobs and career options. Competition for career positions is now at an all-time high while the pool of opportunities is shrinking. The revisualisation of employment and careers further impacts the evolving integration and reliance on technology.

Overall, the clock speed of industry change is continuously increasing as product development lifecycles shorten with digital and process technologies opening market niches and replacing tasks and processes that traditionally took time to establish. The resulting impact is that entry barriers are now lower than ever across many industry sectors.

Therefore, along with Covid-19, a perfect storm has evolved, opening the door to entrepreneurship as a sustainable career move.

Club Entrepreneurship offers you an opportunity to be part of the entrepreneurial 'new breed' that values sustainably and is central to making your ideas real.

MISSION

Club Entrepreneurship brings entrepreneurial educational power to those who want to transform their ideas into a sustainable and purposeful lifestyle and career.

VALUES

The natural environment and all its creatures.

Society and the facilitation of learning and technology for good.

Responsibly creating purposeful economic value.

“That is to embrace your entrepreneurial adventure while being part of the positive change in this world.”

APPROACH

Your learning commences with the first ‘Be An Entrepreneur’ learning course (Course 1), Entrepreneurial Foundations.
Once you complete this course, you will understand more about yourself and your direction. Part of this course will also give you access to research databases, giving a unique insight into the potential of your idea and the marketplace.

When you have completed Course 1, you can then enrol in Course 2. This course, Entrepreneurial Idea Focus, focuses on your idea and prepares the thinking for the market to get ready for your entrepreneurial adventure. You will once again have access to research databases to narrow down your research to pitch your idea to potential influencers and supporters at the course completion.

Once you think about your Entrepreneurial Idea, it is time to consider your business and marketing strategy. Courses 3, 4 and 5 will allow you to understand how to position and market your business to win your customers and succeed in the market in which your business will compete.

Once you have completed Course 1, you will be eligible to join the ‘Club Entrepreneurial’ Club as a subscribed member.
‘The Club’ membership will give you access to professional support, events, other courses specifically tailored to the entrepreneurial journey, and up-to-date market and consumer research. Your other course graduates will also join you to keep you motivated, informed, and focused on your entrepreneurial adventure.

COURSE ENTRY QUALIFICATION

Course entry is not about assessments and qualifications. It is about your attitude and preparedness to work to future-proof your career and build a sustainable business for you, your family, and the planet across the science, technology, and business industries.

If you have the right attitude and want to begin your entrepreneurial journey, then Club Entrepreneurship is for you regardless of where you are in the world.
